Frequently Asked Questions

How does Reviewly analyze app reviews?

Reviewly uses advanced AI algorithms to analyze app reviews from the App Store, providing sentiment analysis, trend identification, and actionable insights to help you understand user feedback patterns.

Can I analyze apps from different countries?

Yes! Reviewly supports global App Store search, allowing you to analyze app reviews from any country and understand regional market differences and user preferences.
