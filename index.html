Frequently Asked Questions

How do I present on an external display?

Connect your iPhone or iPad to a TV or projector using AirPlay or a compatible HDMI/USB‑C adapter. Presently automatically renders a polished presentation with adaptive layout and crisp typography on the external screen.

Which devices are supported?

Presently is designed for iPhone and iPad with interfaces optimized for both. External display presentation works wherever iOS/iPadOS supports screen output.

What media formats can I use?

Use images and videos supported by iOS/iPadOS (for example JPEG/PNG/HEIC and MP4/MOV with H.264/H.265). Add media from Files or your device library for smooth playback and transitions.

How do slideshow timing and video playback work?

Choose auto‑advance with a customizable interval or full manual control. Videos play seamlessly with an optional loop/repeat toggle for continuous playback.

What timers are included?

Use a focused single countdown or manage multiple named timers at once. Set quick presets and adjust durations on the fly during your session.

How does Apple Music integration work?

Select a playlist, view artwork, and control playback right in the app. Apple Music authorization is required, and an active Apple Music subscription may be needed for full functionality.

Is there a first‑launch guide?

Yes. Presently includes a quick walkthrough so you can start presenting fast with the essential setup steps.

How is my data handled?

Your data stays on your device and is not shared with third parties. To remove all data, delete the app and clear related iCloud data if enabled.

How do I contact support?

Contact us at support@karmaacademy.com.au and we’ll be happy to help.
